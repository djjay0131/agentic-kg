# Project Overview

<!-- TODO(phase-b): replace with real 60-second project summary — what the system does, who it's for, and one concrete example. -->

Agentic KG is a platform for building research knowledge graphs from scientific papers and applying AI agents to identify, deduplicate, and advance open research problems.

Research problems are modeled as first-class entities with rich operational context: assumptions, constraints, datasets, metrics, and evidence. Specialized agents operate over the graph to rank problems, propose continuations, execute evaluations, and synthesize results.

## Who it's for

- Researchers looking for tractable open problems in their field
- Research teams tracking problem evolution across papers
- Anyone already using the Denario framework
